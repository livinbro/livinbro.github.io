Using Timers

Why use a timer?

Using a timer on yourself can be a very useful thing, and it can help you know how long you've been playing for, and you can figure out a good time to set for playing.


First off, you can limit yourself by setting a short(ish) timer, and when that timer goes off, take a 30 minute to an hour break, or play for 30 minutes, then take a 5-10 minute break. This can be modified and flexed as the user wants, but it can really help. While your taking a break, you can get up, move around, get a snack or a drink, and even do some small exercises or stretches. There are tons you can do, but setting a limit can really help in the long run, and it's something we all should do.
