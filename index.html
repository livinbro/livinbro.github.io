Video Game Time Efficiency

And how you should manage it

What Exactly are Video Games?

A video game is a lot of things. For some, it's a form of entertainment, or a way to challenge themselves. For others it's a competitive sport they do for a living. What ever it may be, it can be very time consuming, and it might just waste most of your time if your not careful.

Why do they take up time?

Well, the short answer is that they're addicting. VERY addicting. One could spend hours on a single game without even realising it. They'd probably think they've only been on for at most an hour or two, but it'd be much longer than that. Something to try watching the time is with a timer. Or, if you would like to know more about time on games, see the limits.
